The Event

The rebellion is massing on the forest moon of Endor, the location of the secret shield generator protecting central hub of Empire operations, the Death Star. We need you, to join the rebellion and help topple the tyrannical Emperor and his puppets.

Upon landing on the moon, you will be greeted by your squadron commander and directed to your encampment. Upon arriving you will be provided with your schedule, which will vary depending on your package. Package details can be found here.

Ensure that you have logged into your account and that you have prepared the necessary equipment and clothing. Landings commence at dawn (July 5th).

The schedule for the event will be emailed to all ticket holders in advance, however expect to see:

  • Giant paintball battle
  • Speeder rides
  • Nighttime, outdoor screening of Return of the Jedi
  • Photo opportunities with Star Wars characters

Location

The event will in the Redwood National Park in Northern California. Below is a map showing the specific location. For more information, please feel free to contact us at email@email.com

A range of events and stalls will be running throughout the event. Access to certain to certain events or areas of the arena may be limited based upon your ticket package. Tickets to additional events may be purchased separately on the day, subject to availability.
